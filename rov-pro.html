สูตร Win Probability (Logistic Model):
P(A wins) = 1 / (1 + e−Δscore/50) × 100%
Δscore = PowerScore(A) − PowerScore(B) + CounterAdv(A→B)
PowerScore = Σ BaseHeroPower × RoleMultiplier
CounterAdv = Σ counterMatrix[A_hero][B_hero]
เลือกตัวละครเพื่อดูไอเทมแนะนำและการคำนวณ Effective DPS
Effective Damage Formula:
Physical DMG = ATK × Skill_Multi × (1 − Armor/(Armor + 200)) × (1 + CritRate × 1.5)
Magic DMG = AP × Skill_Multi × (1 − MagRes/(MagRes + 200))
DPS = Burst_DMG + (ATK × AS × Attack_Multi × armor_reduction)
Effective HP = HP × (1 + Armor/100) [vs physical]
